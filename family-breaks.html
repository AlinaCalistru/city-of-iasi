Luceafarul Theatre (The Evening Star)

Is Theatre for Children and Youth specialized in plays for families and young audiences, it is also known as as the Puppet Theatre.

It's never to early to introduce kids to the magic of theatre, and Luceafarul Theatre bursting with shows that'll delight junior audiences. There are gentle puppet shows for babies and toddlers.

Rainbow bright, song-stuffed adventures for young kids. Smart dramas that are sure to hold teenagers rapt. And spectacular musicals that'll get the whole family on side.

Every year, in October, the venue hosts the "International Theatre Festival for Young Audience"

Kidsland indoor play

Indoor play Kidsland - has been designed with both parents and children in mind and our passion for excellence and high standards ensure both a relaxing and enjoyable visit for all the family. So come along and see for yourself what everyone is talking about and have some fun!

Activities in the playground: all events (celebrations, creative or recreational activities) are organized by the animators thoroughly prepared for meeting with the little ones; they are always willing to initiate and guide children in the arts of play, storytelling, laughter, dance, song, theater, drawing and many other arts.

Children will be discreetly and attentively supervised by qualified staff so that fun can be performed safely without affecting the mood of the little ones.

Unquenchable and joyful characters will give unforgettable moments to all who will cross the threshold of this place of guides.
